About A-Technologies

Company Overview

A-Technologies is a US-based technology consulting and implementation partner. We provide skilled consultants to vendor companies and act as an Employer of Record (EOR) for professionals working across the United States.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to empower organizations by connecting them with top-tier technical talent and providing seamless implementation support. We envision a world where technology projects are delivered with excellence and consultants flourish in their careers.

What We Do

As an implementation partner, we collaborate closely with vendors to ensure project milestones are met with high quality. Through our EOR services, consultants gain access to payroll, benefits, and compliance support while working with different clients.
